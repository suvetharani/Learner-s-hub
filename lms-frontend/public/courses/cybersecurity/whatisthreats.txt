What Are Security Threats?
--------------------------

In cybersecurity, a "threat" is any potential danger that can exploit a vulnerability
to compromise the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of information or systems.

Common examples:
- Malware and viruses
- Phishing attacks
- Denial-of-Service (DoS) attacks
- Insider threats
